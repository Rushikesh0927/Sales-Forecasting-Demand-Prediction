Prediction Limitations & Model Accuracy Window

  • Optimal prediction period: 2010 - 2013. The model was trained on data from Feb 2010 to Oct 2012, so predictions within this window are most reliable. Dates after 2013 will have decreasing accuracy as economic conditions (CPI, fuel prices) diverge from training data.
  • For dates before 2010 or after 2013, treat predictions as rough estimates only. The further from the training window, the less reliable the output.
  • Lag features (recent sales history) use historical store-department averages as proxies. In a production system, these would come from a live sales database.
  • The model predicts at the department level (not individual product level). Each department may contain many product categories.
  • External factors like local events, weather extremes, promotions, and pandemic effects are not captured in the model.
  • Predictions show a confidence indicator (High / Medium / Low) based on how much historical data exists for the selected store-department combination.
